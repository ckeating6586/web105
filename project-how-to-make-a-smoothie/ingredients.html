Protein Powder

You'll need 1 scoop of protein. Protein is essential for building muscle or maintaining muscle in your body.

Bananas

Use half of a banana. Bananas are a good source of vitamin B6, Fiber, Potassium, Magnesium, Vitamin C, and Manganese.

Almond Milk

Measure out one cup of almond milk. There's no cholesterol or saturated fat in almond milk so it keeps your heart healthy. Almond milk has both vitamin D and C which can reduce the risk of osteoporosis. Almond milk doesn't have lactose in it so it's a great alternative for those who are lactose intolerant and even a good vegan option.

Strawberries

You'll want a cup of strawberries. Strawberries have a lot of vitamin C in them which helps your immune system. Strawberries can reduce the risk of heart attacks because they contain anthocyanins. They can also help to reduce inflammation.
