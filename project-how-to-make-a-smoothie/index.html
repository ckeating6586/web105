Benefits Of A Protein Smoothie

  • Protein smoothies can help reduce fat mass and improve physical performance
  • Can aid in weightloss
  • Can improve physical endurance
  • Can reduce hunger and improve glycemic control

Benefits Of Protein

  • Helps preserve lean muscle mass and increases satiety
  • Can help improve blood sugar levels
  • It can curb hunger and boost your metabolism

When Should You Have A Protein Smoothie

Protein smoothies can be enjoyed at any time of day. You can use them as meal replacements if you are trying to lose or maintain a certain weight. You can also use them as a recovery drink after a workout.


How Do You Pick Your Ingredients?

I generally like to pick a variety of ingredients. I like to choose a fruit that tends to be on the sweet side and a fruit that is on the tarte side. I feel that having both a sweet and a tarte, creates a better balance of flavor. I sometimes like to add a cup of green leafy vegetables, like spinach or kale.



I always choose a protein powder to add into my smoothies because I notice I never get enough protein in my day.
