Rathaus Glockenspiel

In Germany, I stayed in the center of Munich. Everyday at 11am, 12pm, and 5pm the large clock chimed and little figurines danced in a circle.

Neuschwanstein Castle

The Neuschwanstein Castle was an epic site to see. The Disney castle was modeled after this castle.

Black Forest

Black Forest is a beautiful area of Germany that is home to some of the best cuckoo clock makers.
